FANTASY Escape from the
Dragon's lair

On your adventure to find Aurarian, a rare element found within the galaxy, you and your group decide to rest inside planet Argorian's caves. As the hums of your spaceship calm you to sleep, you are suddenly awoken with a burning pain on your arm, followed by a large roar. You find yourself in the air, under a hearty dragon. In the following few minutes of fading in and out of unconsciousness, your space crew is taken prisoner by a blood thirsty dragon, and is therefore stuck in a musty lair that which emanates the smells of broken dreams. Within 60 minutes, using the various clues in the lair, you are to escape to achieve your liberation from the dragons.

This room requires 3 to 8 players.
Cost: $18

SCI-FI The Black Hole

On the journey to find a habitable bearing planet for your species, you and your space crew is hijacked by interdimensional pirates. The mischievous looters, having snuck relaxive sedatives into your bloodstream, therefore putting you to sleep, have sabotaged your ship to fly straight into the event horizon of a nearby black hole in hopes of entertainment. Additionally, all of your safety equipment have been deactivated. In order to turn the ship back on and redirect your ship, your team must manually open the door to the power room. Unfortunately, 3 safety deadlocks have been placed on the door. Your team is to find the codes placed by the troublesome pirates to save the crew.



This room requires 3 to 6 players.
Cost: $24
